+ Are you an Active Traveller?

Faster. Higher. Stronger.

We’ve hunted high and low in search of adventures that engage the muscles as much as the mind and heart. Biking, hiking, whitewater rafting, dog-sledding, and other holy-moly-I-can’t-believe-I-did-that activities await.

Local transport

Self-propelled travel for the scenic stretches, local rides (buses, taxis, trains, etc) on the longer routes to save time.

Character-rich lodgings

We pick authentic accommodations that match the environments, from whitewashed Spanish haciendas to camping under the stars on Kilimanjaro.

Skilled CEOs & Expert assistance

Your trip’s Chief Experience Officer (CEO) is your guide, translator, coach, historian, bike mechanic, and friend all rolled into one expert package.

Take it to the limit

“Active” covers everything from pleasant mountain strolls and leisurely bike rides through rolling countryside to challenging hikes and demanding off-road rides.

What you get out of an Active trip entirely depends on how much you want to put into it.

Learn about our Physical Rating

Physical Rating

Each trip is assigned a Physical Demand rating to give you an idea of how strenuous the activity will be before you hit the trail.

All G Adventures trips feature a Physical Demand rating from one to five. All Active trips are rated at three or higher.

More

It’s a journey, not a race

You don’t need to be an elite endurance athlete to enjoy an Active trip. Many of our itineraries are built for the first-time active traveller and offer a more relaxed (but still athletic) experience.

We’ll provide you with training guidelines when you book to help you prepare for your specific adventure, and if you’re unsure whether a trip is right for your skill level, just ask and we’ll get your sorted out.
